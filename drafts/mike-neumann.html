About Mike

The Heart Behind Adobe's Content Supply Chain

Based in the Greater Ottawa area, Mike Neumann has spent more than 25 years at Adobe, rising through the ranks to become the Sr. Director of Content & Collaboration. Over that journey he has led the organization's entire content supply chain, shaped content and workflow sales practices, and built a thriving business around helping enterprises transform how they create, manage, and deliver content at scale.

Mike's impact reaches well beyond revenue numbers. He is widely recognized as a mentor, coach, and deal closer who lifts everyone around him. From guiding new hires through their first quarters to coaching seasoned sellers through complex enterprise engagements, Mike has been the kind of leader people remember for decades.

He is a proud Canadian through and through, bringing warmth, humility, and that unmistakable resilience to everything he does.
